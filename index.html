About Us

The Barani Institute of Information Technology(BIIT) was established at the university, in 1998, after a long and deliberate consideration of the growing needs of Software Pakistan, known as Public Private Partnership.The institute was originally established as a joint venture between the PMAS Arid Agriculture University and private sector.

  • BIIT takes pride in being the first institute in Pakistan to launch four-year CS and AI degree programs in 1998 and 1999 respectively, which recognized nationally and internationally.

  • Currently BIIT focuses on comprehensive undergraduate degree program, namely: BS(CS), with specialization in General Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

  • Very soon two more specializations, Data Science and Cyber Security are also going to be launched.
